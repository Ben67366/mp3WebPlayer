Place your sample files here for the 'サンプルを試す' button:

- sample.mp3  (required for audio)
- sample.srt  (optional - will be auto-loaded as subtitles if present)

The sample button in waveform-player.html will automatically load:
  ./sample/sample.mp3   as audio
  ./sample/sample.srt   as subtitles (if the file exists)
